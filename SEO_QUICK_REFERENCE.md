## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. ❌ Keyword stuffing in meta tags
   - ✅ Do: Keep titles natural and human-readable
   
2. ❌ Duplicate meta tags across pages
   - ✅ Do: Unique title and description per page
   
3. ❌ Ignoring mobile optimization
   - ✅ Do: Test all pages on mobile (Next.js handles this)
   
4. ❌ Slow page load times
   - ✅ Do: Run Lighthouse audit monthly
   
5. ❌ No internal linking strategy
   - ✅ Do: Link area pages → service pages → blog
   
6. ❌ Inconsistent business information
   - ✅ Do: Use siteConfig for all NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
   
7. ❌ Outdated or broken links
   - ✅ Do: Check for 404s in Search Console monthly
